## [Everyone's a thought leader. Almost no one is thinking.](https://bernste.in/writings/everyones-a-thought-leader-almost-no-one-is-thinking/)

**Summary**
Michael Bernstein observes that LinkedIn has become a performance of expertise rather than a place where expertise is actually demonstrated. The dominant pattern is: find something trending, hand it to an AI to repackage, post it as your own insight. This produces output that looks like thinking but is not. His core claim: AI amplifies whatever intellectual effort you put in — if the input is zero, the output is still zero, just louder. His prescription is to build the habit of thinking independently first (reading, writing in a notebook, sitting with a problem) before reaching for any AI tool.

**Key takeaways**
- "AI is the acceleration. Your thinking is the mass." — without genuine input, AI amplification produces nothing worth amplifying.
- The thought leader playbook (copy trending idea → run through AI → post) is now detectable and devalued because everyone does it.
- The counter-move is analogue: read originals, keep a notebook, think before prompting.
